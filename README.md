## Project: Circular Transformation & SBM Transformation Strategy (Unilever)
**Files:** `Group01_Team05_ SBM_Capgemini.pdf` (Strategic SBM Framework) & `Group 01_Team05_Unilever_Presentation.pdf` (Benchmark & Heat Map Evaluation)

A deep-dive corporate restructuring strategy for **Unilever**, shifting its global consumer goods portfolio from disposable packaging and extractive sourcing into an integrated, ecologically sustainable growth model.

### Core Business & SBM Innovation Insights
* **Slowing Strategy (Waterless Formats):** Structured the transition of high-volume liquid items (soaps, shampoos, detergents) into solid, concentrated, or powder form factor sheets. This eliminates single-use plastic packaging, slashes shipping logistical overhead, and introduces reusable glass home-dispensers to change consumer behavior loops.
* **Closing Strategy (Bulk Refill Systems):** Built feasibility models for storefront bulk-refill integration, calculating the scalability, initial capital expenditure hurdles, multi-stakeholder supply chain coordination risks, and long-term customer loyalty retention metrics.
* **Regenerating Strategy (Biodiversity Corridors):** Designed a pioneering approach going beyond standard corporate certifications (like the Rainforest Alliance) to directly fund and orchestrate indigenous NGO-led rainforest, mangrove, and wetland restoration corridors. This hedges Unilever against supply shocks, mitigates biosphere integrity boundary risks, and builds structural brand differentiation.
* **Feasibility & Scalability Heat Mapping:** Evaluated each strategy against strict corporate governance matrices, identifying critical friction factors (e.g., consumer inertia, short-term margin viability, sensory reluctance) vs. growth drivers (reduced recurring packaging spend, localized risk mitigation, structural ESG alignment).

### Key Competencies Demonstrated
* Sustainable Business Model (SBM) Innovation (Slowing, Closing, & Regenerating Circularity)
* Corporate Environmental Benchmarking & Strategic Scalability Mapping
* Planetary Boundary Risk Mitigation (Freshwater Use, Climate Change, Biosphere Integrity)
